Special Report: WILDFIRES

With climate change wildfires have become more severe over the last 15 years; they will intensify in the next 20 to 50 years.

Wildfire experts predict that by 2041, there will be four large, high-intensity forest fires for every three that occur now, with the number of days when conditions are conducive to fires increasing.

In some regions of the world countries have decided to mix modern science and indigenous knowledge for a better management.

Data collected by NASA shows the outbreaks of wildfires around the world and the damage they have wrought.
